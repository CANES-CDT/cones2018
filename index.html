Conference on Non-Equilibrium Systems

King's College London

25-27th June 2018

"Frontiers of non-equilibrium science"

  • CONES will bring together leading experts in non-equilibrium science
  • Thematic strands will include recent advances in the physical sciences, biological modelling, and statistical mechanics
  • The conference will explore common challenges in non-equilibrium systems

We are also pleased to announce a free lecture to accompany the conference which is open to everyone. This lecture by Dr Allan Tucker is entitled Three AI Algorithms Inspired by Data from the Life Sciences and will take place on Tue 26th June. Guests not already attending the main conference should register here or see our poster for more information.
